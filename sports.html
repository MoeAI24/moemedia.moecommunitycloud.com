Jimmy Butler Out for Season After Torn ACL

Warriors•Jan 20, 2026•Impact Breakdown

Golden State loses more than a scorer — they lose late-clock problem solving, physical pressure, and a defensive matchup eraser.

By the Numbers: what Golden State loses

Scoring & usage: Butler absorbed high-leverage possessions without tanking efficiency — those late-clock touches get redistributed, usually at a lower success rate.

Efficiency: fewer paint attacks and fewer free throws means more jump-shot-dependent offense over time.

Defense: losing the toughest wing assignment increases switching stress, help rotations, and open-corner looks.

Clutch minutes: close games get harder — responsibility spreads, and clutch efficiency typically drops when the safety valve disappears.

Bottom line: you can replace minutes — you can’t replace impact density.
